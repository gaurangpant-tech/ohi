Privacy Policy

Revised on: 15th September 2025

Overview

O hi is a consumer social app. By using our services you agree to our collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. For sensitive categories of information we will request your explicit consent. If you have questions, contact us at contact@ohiapp.com.

Information We Collect

We collect three main categories of information: information you provide; information we obtain when you use our services; and information we receive from third parties. Examples include account details (name, username, email, phone), profile data, photos and videos you upload, logs of usage, device and sensor data, and optional precise geolocation when you consent.

Information You Provide

When you create an account or complete your profile you provide personal details such as name, username, email, phone number, gender, and date of birth. You may also provide photos, videos, and other profile details (personality, interests). Sensitive personal data will require explicit consent.

Usage & Device Information

We collect usage information (features used, searches, clicks, interactions), device information (IP address, device ID, OS, browser, advertising IDs), and optional sensor data to improve features (e.g., location-based chatrooms). Chat content is end-to-end stored on devices and we do not access it except when required by law or with consent.

Contact List & Sharing

Access to your contact list is optional. If granted, it enables invites, link sharing and chatroom invites. We collect contact names and phone numbers only for those features and do not share them with third parties except as required by law or with your consent.

How We Use & Share Information

Your information is used to operate and improve the service, administer accounts, provide customer support, recommend connections, link devices for a consistent experience, and display relevant ads. We may share data with service providers, business partners, or in legal circumstances. We may also share aggregated or de-identified information.

Data Retention & Control

We retain basic account information until you request deletion. Some data (including backups or data required by law) may be retained for a limited period. You can access, correct, revoke permissions, or request deletion via the app or by contacting support.

Security

We take steps such as encryption and restricted access to protect your information, but no system is perfect — you should secure your devices and be cautious sharing sensitive details.

Children

Our services are not directed to anyone under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.
